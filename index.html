On Pan

The Great God Pan, not in charge nor charged with taking care of mortals. More of a god to set an example for which us hairless apes can never duplicate but maybe replicate in our own ways. At some point we fell off, trying to master the nature instead of trying to invigorate it to grow beyond our powers.
