The Great God Pan

where the veil goes thin and the ink runs strange

Enter if you dare to look sideways at the world.

This is a place for writing that does not behave — fiction, prose, verse, and testimony from those who have stood at the edge of something they cannot quite name and had the nerve to write it down anyway.

The Great God Pan does not ask for credentials. He asks only that your work be honest, strange, and alive.
